Finance Controls Guide

Invoice intake
- Accepted invoice formats are PDF and structured CSV exports from approved vendors.
- Image-only screenshots are not accepted for processing.

Case routing
- Billing disputes under $250 remain with the support queue.
- Disputes of $250 or more, chargeback requests, and tax correction cases must be handed to finance.

Review cadence
- Control documents are reviewed on the first business day of each quarter.

Mailing address
- Physical returns requiring finance inspection should be sent to 4100 Metric Drive, Suite 220, Austin, TX 78758.

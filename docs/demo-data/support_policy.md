# Support Policy Reference

## Password reset handling

Standard password reset requests submitted through the support queue should be completed within 30 minutes during staffed hours.

If the account belongs to an executive, finance approver, or system administrator, the request must be verified by phone before the reset is completed.

## Call recording retention

Call recordings are retained for 180 days in the support archive. Legal holds override the standard retention period.

## Onboarding exceptions

The Customer Operations team owns onboarding exceptions for standard customers. Cases involving contractual pricing or non-standard tax treatment must be handed to Revenue Operations for review.

## Policy update approvals

The Director of Support Operations approves changes to the support handling policy after review from the Compliance partner.
